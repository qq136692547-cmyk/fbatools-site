About

Why FBATools exists

Most "Amazon FBA calculators" online were built before April 2024. They don't include the new Inbound Placement Fee or the Low-Inventory Fee — and together those two take 5–10% of your margin. Sellers using those tools are pricing products 10% too cheap.

FBATools includes both fees by default. The fee table is verified against Amazon's 2026 published schedule and updated whenever Amazon announces a change.
