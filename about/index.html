About FBATools

FBATools is a small, independent tool site built for Amazon FBA sellers. We help you calculate the real cost of selling on Amazon — including every fee Amazon charges in 2026 — so you can price products with confidence.

What we offer

  • Amazon FBA Calculator — Full fee breakdown including 2026 Inbound Placement and Low-Inventory fees.
  • FBA vs FBM Calculator — Side-by-side profit comparison.
  • More tools — Coming soon: eBay Fee Calculator, Etsy Fee Calculator, Amazon Keyword Research.

Our principles

  1. 100% free, no signup. No account, no email, no credit card. Open and use.
  2. Privacy first. All calculations run in your browser. We do not log or transmit your financial data.
  3. Up to date. Fee tables refreshed whenever Amazon publishes a new schedule.
  4. Independent. We are not affiliated with Amazon.com. Amazon, FBA, and the Amazon logo are trademarks of Amazon.com, Inc.
