Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the FBATools calculator?

FBATools uses Amazon's published 2026 fee schedules, including the new Inbound Placement Fee (effective April 2024) and the Low-Inventory Fee. Actual Amazon invoices may vary by ±2% due to dimensional weight rounding, aged inventory surcharges, and product-specific storage rules.

Does FBATools work for non-US Amazon marketplaces?

Currently we support the US marketplace. UK, EU, JP, CA and AU marketplaces are coming next.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All calculations happen in your browser. We do not log, store, or transmit any of your financial inputs.

Do I need an Amazon Seller account to use it?

No signup, no account, no Amazon login. Open the page and start calculating.
