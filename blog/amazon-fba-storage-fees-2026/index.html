Amazon FBA Storage Fees 2026: Monthly & Long-Term Costs Guide

Storage fees are the FBA cost that sneaks up on sellers. Unlike referral or fulfillment fees that hit per-order, storage accumulates monthly — and the day your inventory ages past 181 days, the surcharge can wipe out the profit from your slow movers entirely.

2026 Monthly Storage Rates

PeriodStandard-sizeOversize
January - September$0.78/cu ft$0.56/cu ft
October - December (Q4)$2.40/cu ft$1.40/cu ft

Q4 rates are roughly 3× higher than the rest of the year. A product that costs $0.20/unit in off-peak storage can jump to $0.60+ in Q4. Sellers who over-ship for Q4 often find their storage bill exceeding their Q4 advertising budget.

Source: Amazon Seller Central — FBA Storage Fees

Aged Inventory Surcharge

AgeSurcharge Rate
181 - 365 days$1.50/cu ft (added to monthly storage)
365+ days$6.90/cu ft or $0.15/unit (whichever is greater)

This is the fee that turns a break-even product into a loss. A case of 24 units sitting in Amazon's warehouse for 200 days incurs the monthly storage fee plus the $1.50/cu ft surcharge. By day 365, the surcharge jumps to $6.90/cu ft — more than the base storage fee itself.

Amazon sends automated warnings at 90 and 180 days. If you receive a 180-day notice and your margin on that product is under 20%, running a liquidation sale is usually cheaper than paying another 6 months of surcharge.

The same product costs 5× more in storage if it sits for 6 months instead of 2. This is why inventory velocity is the most overlooked variable in FBA profitability.

How to Minimize Storage Fees

  • Ship for 30-60 days of sales, not 90+. Amazon penalizes slow inventory, not frequent restocking. Multiple small shipments cost more in inbound shipping but save significantly in storage fees.
  • Use the Inventory Age tool in Seller Central monthly. Products approaching 90 and 180 days need a decision: promote, discount, or liquidate.
  • Seasonal products ship later than you think. Sending Q4 inventory in August means paying 3 months of off-peak storage before the Q4 rush. Send in October instead.
  • Use the FBA Calculator to model storage costs before sourcing a product. A slow-moving item with a 20% margin on paper may be unprofitable after 6 months of storage.
