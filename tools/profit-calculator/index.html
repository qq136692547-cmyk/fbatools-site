When FBA wins, when FBM wins

01 / decision guide
[ FBA ]

FBA wins when

Your product is small, light, turns fast, and Prime shipping gives you a Buy Box boost that outweighs the $3-6 FBA fee. Typical for items under 1 lb and under $40.

[ FBM ]

FBM wins when

Your product is large, fragile, has long shelf life, or your self-fulfilled shipping is under $4. Also useful for testing a new SKU without locking up inventory fees.

[ ∞ ]

The break-even

For a 0.75 lb item in Home & Kitchen, FBM starts to win when your outbound shipping drops below ~$3.50. Below that, FBA's $4.98 + placement + storage + low-inventory fees outweigh Prime's Buy Box benefit.
