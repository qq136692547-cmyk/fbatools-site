When does FBA beat FBM (and vice versa)?

FBA wins when: your product is small/light, turns fast, and Prime shipping drives a Buy Box win rate boost that outweighs the $3-6 FBA fee. Typical for items under 1 lb and under $40.

FBM wins when: your product is large/fragile/oversized, has long shelf life, or your self-fulfilled shipping is under $4. Also useful for testing a new SKU without locking up inventory fees.
