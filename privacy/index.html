Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24.

Summary

FBATools does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of the data you enter into our calculators. All calculations happen locally in your browser.

What we don't collect

  • No financial inputs (sale price, COGS, weights) — calculations are 100% client-side.
  • No account, no email, no name.
  • No IP-based tracking beyond anonymous aggregate analytics.

What we do collect

  • Aggregate, anonymized analytics (page views, referrer) via a privacy-respecting tool. We use this only to understand which calculators are useful and to prioritize improvements.
  • Standard server logs retained briefly for security and abuse prevention. Logs are deleted after 30 days.

Cookies

FBATools does not set any non-essential cookies. If we ever add advertising (e.g. Google AdSense in the future), that will be opt-in where required by law and disclosed here.

Contact

Privacy questions? Open an issue on our public GitHub repository.
